✅ Top Interview Key Points – Factory Method Pattern (Burger Example)

Interface-Based Factory Design
→ BurgerFactory ek interface hai jo product creation (createBurger) ko abstract karta hai – har concrete factory apni family ke products banati hai.

Encapsulation of Object Creation
→ Object creation logic har factory class (SighBurger, KingBurger) ke andar chhupa hua hai – client ko sirf factory call karna hota hai.

Open/Closed Principle Followed
→ Naya burger add karna ho to nayi class banao, existing code ko touch nahi karna padta (OCP – Open for extension, closed for modification).

Family of Related Products
→ SighBurger normal buns ke burgers banata hai, KingBurger wheat buns ke – clearly separate product families define ki gayi hai.

Scalability & Maintenance Easy
→ Har burger type ka creation alag factory se ho raha hai, isse future me naye variants add karna super easy ho jata hai.
